# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

We are committed to acting and interacting in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and
healthy project environment.

## Our Standards

Positive behavior:

- Empathy and kindness toward others
- Respect for differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Graceful giving and receiving of constructive feedback
- Responsibility and learning from mistakes
- Focus on what is best for the project

Unacceptable behavior:

- Sexualized language or imagery; sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments; personal or political attacks
- Harassment, public or private
- Publishing others' private information (physical or email address) without permission
- Other conduct reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement Responsibilities

I enforce this Code of Conduct and act on behavior I judge inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.

I reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate
this Code of Conduct, and will explain moderation decisions where appropriate.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and also applies when an individual is officially representing
the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Report abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior to [report@jekwwer.com][report@jekwwer.com]. I review all
complaints promptly and confidentially.

## Enforcement Guidelines

I apply these escalating consequences for violations:

### 1. Correction

**Impact**: Minor, unprofessional, or unwelcome behavior.

**Consequence**: Private written warning explaining the violation. Public apology may be requested.

### 2. Warning

**Impact**: Significant or repeated violation.

**Consequence**: Warning plus limited interaction in project spaces for a set period. Continued violations escalate to
temporary or permanent ban.

### 3. Temporary Ban

**Impact**: Serious or sustained inappropriate behavior.

**Consequence**: Temporary ban from project spaces; no interaction with maintainer or project during this period.
Violations escalate to permanent ban.

### 4. Permanent Ban

**Impact**: Repeated serious violations, harassment, or aggression.

**Consequence**: Permanent ban from project spaces.
